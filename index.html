How It Works

Speaking in four simple steps

No complicated setup. No learning curve. Just download and start communicating.

1

Choose Your Mode

Select Adult or Child mode during setup. This customizes touch targets, grid size, and visual style.

2

Build a Sentence

Tap symbols on the communication board. They appear in your sentence strip at the top, building your message.

3

Tap to Speak

Hit the speak button and VoiceAAC reads your sentence aloud — in your Personal Voice or a system voice.

4

Get Faster

AI predictions learn your patterns. Quick phrases save your favorites. Communication speeds up every day.

Personal Voice

Your words. Your voice.

Apple's Personal Voice lets you create a voice that sounds like you — set up right on your iPhone. VoiceAAC uses it to speak your sentences, so your communication sounds like you, not a robot.

1

Open iPhone Settings

Go to Accessibility → Personal Voice and follow Apple's guided setup (about 15 minutes).

2

Grant VoiceAAC Permission

Open VoiceAAC and allow it to use your Personal Voice when prompted.

3

Speak as You

Every sentence you build now speaks in your own voice. Your identity, preserved.
